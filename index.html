About our methodology

Cost? We will only charge if we can reduce your investment. You can only save money!

How do we do it Most suppliers use a ratio power/m² to size your Project. We perform a detailed and specific calculation, room by room for your Project. As we deliver you the detailed calculations, you are able to check we recommend you the right sizing, while keeping safety margins. We don’t downsize putting your confort at risk.

Trust? we are totally independents. No fee from product suppliers nor from installers.

What do we need from you

  • A full copy of the air conditionning offer you received.

  • Your exact address.

  • Building drawings with details: floor plans, sections, detailed drawings, windows specifications, walls/roofs/floors insulation, lighting drawings, ventilation.

  • You don’t know what to send, send us everything, we’ll find all we need. We’ll be in touch in case that we need further information.

What do we need from you

Improved comfort. Right-sized air conditioners deliver smaller volumes of air over longer periods of time.

Better humidity control. In order for air conditioners to dehumidify, they have to cycle long enough for moisture to condense on the coils and drain away. Quieter home. Noise reduction: less start & stop, air moves slower through ducts and registers, smaller compressor and fan motors.

Lower energy bills. Oversized AC prevent air conditioners from operating at peak efficiency. Thus, right-sized units provide an equivalent amount of space cooling for less energy consumed. Fewer maintenance problems. The maintenance frequency can be increased while lives can be shortened.
